STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Document: SOP-MNT-001
Equipment: Centrifugal Pump XP-500
Department: Mechanical Maintenance
Version: 3.2
Effective Date: 2024-01-15

1.0 PURPOSE
This procedure outlines the maintenance requirements for centrifugal pumps.

2.0 SCOPE
Applies to all XP-500 series pumps in the processing plant.

3.0 PROCEDURE
3.1 Daily Inspection
- Check pressure gauge readings (normal: 5-7 bar)
- Listen for unusual bearing noise
- Verify oil level in sight glass
- Record readings in maintenance log

3.2 Weekly Maintenance
- Lubricate all grease fittings
- Check belt tension (deflection: 10-15mm)
- Clean air intake filters
- Inspect coupling alignment

3.3 Monthly Service
- Full system inspection
- Replace worn mechanical seals
- Calibrate pressure sensors
- Test emergency shutdown
- Update maintenance records

4.0 SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
- Lockout/Tagout before maintenance
- Wear PPE (gloves, safety glasses, steel-toe boots)
- Depressurize system before opening
